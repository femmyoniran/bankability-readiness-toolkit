Assessment Framework

◆

Bankability Scoring

Composite scoring model evaluating five dimensions: technology viability, financial strength, credit risk, project structure, and market conditions. Weighted scores produce an overall bankability readiness rating from 0 to 100.

▮

Financial Modeling

Full pro forma cash flow projections with DSCR, IRR, NPV, LCOE, and payback period. Includes MACRS depreciation, ITC/PTC tax credit modeling, and debt amortization schedules over the full project life.

▲

Credit Risk Analysis

Probability of default estimation, loss given default assessment, and expected loss calculation. Counterparty risk scoring with equivalent credit rating mapping and risk factor identification.

▬

Sensitivity Analysis

One-factor-at-a-time sensitivity on revenue, operating costs, capital costs, interest rates, and capacity factor. Shows impact on DSCR, IRR, and NPV across downside and upside scenarios.

□

USDA RUS Form 201

Generates structured data aligned with the RUS electric loan program application. Covers borrower information, cost estimates, financial feasibility, and environmental review sections required for Form 201 submission.

●

DOE LPO Title XVII

Pre-application and full application data templates for the Loan Programs Office Title XVII innovative energy loan guarantee. Includes credit subsidy estimation, technology innovation narrative, and compliance requirements.
